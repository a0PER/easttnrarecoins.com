Who We Are

East Tennessee Rare Coins is a Numismatic firm owned and operated by professional Coin Dealer and ANA member, Bo Mucciarone. With over a decade in the field we cater to our clients on an appointment only basis to ensure adequate time for key details and accuracy while giving personalized attention and privacy. Located in Cleveland TN, we also serve clients from Chattanooga, Athens, North Georgia and Southeast Tennessee.

Most of our clients have the same Questions
  • What do I have?
  • What is it worth?
  • Who can I trust?

East Tennessee Rare Coins can assist in every single step. Every collection we purchase is carefully reviewed by a professional devoted to the highest ethical standards. Honesty, Transparency, and Integrity are our Core Values. Our By Appointment Only model means a one-on-one meeting where we review every coin together — you learn the history, stories, and value of each piece, resulting in a fair and honest appraisal. Our Extensive Network of industry connections allows us to present unbeatable cash offers.

Reach Us

We proudly serve clients across Cleveland TN, Chattanooga TN, Athens TN, Ooltewah TN and the surrounding Southeast Tennessee region. All meetings are by appointment only — call or message us to arrange a private, one-on-one consultation.
