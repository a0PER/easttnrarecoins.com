Who We Are

East Tennessee Rare Coins is a Numismatic firm owned and operated by professional Coin Dealer and ANA member, Bo Mucciarone. With over a decade in the field we cater to our clients on an appointment only basis to ensure that we have adequate time for key details and accuracy all while giving personalized attention and privacy to all of our clients. Located out of Cleveland TN, we also deal with estates and collections from Chattanooga, Athens, North Georgia through Southeast Tennessee and the surrounding areas.
